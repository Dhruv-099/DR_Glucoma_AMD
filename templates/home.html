About Us

Our group is working on a project to help doctors diagnose Diabetic Retinopathy, Glaucoma, and AMD using machine learning algorithms.

Eye Diseases We Focus On

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD) affects the macula, the central part of the retina responsible for sharp vision. It can lead to blurred or distorted central vision, with two types: dry and wet AMD.

Glaucoma

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often due to high eye pressure. It can cause gradual vision loss, sometimes without noticeable symptoms, which makes early detection crucial.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic Retinopathy is caused by damage to the blood vessels in the retina due to diabetes. It can lead to vision impairment or blindness if not managed properly, but early detection can prevent its progression.
